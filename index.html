Sai Gattupalli

Hello and namasté.

I am Sai and I am a third year PhD student in the Learning Sciences program at the College of Education at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I am located at the Advanced Learning Technologies Lab (N137 Furcolo) in Amherst, MA and work with Dr. Ivon Arroyo.

I study culture, learning through play, and computational thinking (CT). I mainly aim to broaden the focus on cultural contexts and its influence on K12 CT skills in the developing nations (such as India), with regard to socio-cultural and game-based learning theories. Through my researches, I hope to translate this research agenda into educational policy, focused on benefiting developing nations' STEM educational systems.
